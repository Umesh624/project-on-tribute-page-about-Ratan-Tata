About Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist, and former chairman of Tata Sons. He has played a pivotal role in transforming the Tata Group into a global powerhouse. Known for his humility, integrity, and forward-thinking leadership, Ratan Tata continues to inspire millions around the world.

Philanthropy

Ratan Tata is deeply committed to philanthropy, dedicating over 60% of Tata Sons' profits to charitable causes through the Tata Trusts. His efforts include improving healthcare, education, and rural development in India. He also made significant contributions to COVID-19 relief and research.
